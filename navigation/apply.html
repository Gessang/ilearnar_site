Join Ilearnar team as a tutor

Read through carefully before applying

Thank you for your interest to join the Ilearnar team as a tutor. We're excited to have passionate educators like you contribute to our mission of empowering learners through quality language instructions.
As an Ilearnar tutor, you'll have the opportunity to share your knowledge, inspire learners, and make a positive impact on their language journey. ... Also, you'll have access to a range of resources and support mechanisms to enhance your teaching skills and professional development. We offer ongoing training, workshops, and materials designed to help you deliver engaging and effective lessons.


Benefits:

1. We connect you with learners for both virtual and in-person classes.

2. We set up a center for you if your physical classroom learners are up to ten.

3. You are paid based on the number of learners and hours. (More details on payment will be made available to you once you're selected).

4. We provide ongoing training, workshops, and resources to support tutors in their professional growth.

5. Network with teachers across the world and have access to career development opportunities.


Basic requirements:

1. Communication and Interpersonal skills

2. ND, NCE, Degree, Masters, P.hd

3. Must be up to 20 years and above

4. Minimum of 1 year teaching experience

5. Proficiency in English communication

6. Valid means of Identification

7. Smartphone with internet access

8. Knowledge of virtual classroom setup (added advantage)


Requirements for adult english literacy tutors:

1. Knowledge of adult learning principles (you'll be taught after being selected)

2. Minimum of NCE or it equivalent

3. Minimum of two years teaching experience

4. Basic knowledge of instructional design principles (added advantage).

5. Age 23 and above


Requirements for advanced communication tutors:

1. Advanced command of English communication (written and verbal)

2. Public speaking skills

3. Presentation skills

4. English language degree or its equivalent will be an added advantage

5. Professional certificate in communication, negotiation or public speaking will be an added advantage

6. Minimum age of 23 years

If you meet any category of the above requirements, click on the application button below to complete the application form. We look forward to learning more about you and your teaching expertise.

Best regards!
